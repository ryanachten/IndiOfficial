about

'indi' is the moniker of contemporary musician & composer Indira Force,
who originates from the rain forests of Titirangi, New Zealand,
& built her foundations in classical piano.

Indi writes using a combination of electronic and classical instrumentation, with a signature sound that relies on intricate details and multi-layered structures. She released her debut album 'Precipice' on September 22nd 2017, comprising of 8 eclectic tracks that she had composed and produced over the course of two and a half years.

Indi is also known for her work with the Christchurch based trip-hop band Doprah [Arch Hill Recordings], which garnered international traction and acclaim at the time. She started with the group as a co-writer at only 19 in 2013, and they were nominated for awards at VNZMA the following year.

Indi is also a composer for screen and continues to work more so within this field than any other. Her work in film often contains string arrangement-based themes or ambient-electronic elements.
